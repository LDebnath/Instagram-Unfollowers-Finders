# Instagram Unfollower Finder

[![GitHub Pages](https://img.shields.io/badge/GitHub-Pages-blue?logo=github)](https://ldebnath.github.io/Instagram-Unfollowers-Finders/)

A **free, browser-based tool** to find Instagram users who don’t follow you back (“Not Following”) and your loyal fans. Fully **client-side**, works offline, and supports **dark/light mode**.

**Live Demo:** [Instagram Unfollower Finder](https://ldebnath.github.io/Instagram-Unfollowers-Finders/)

---


## Features

- Find **Fans**: Users who follow you but you don’t follow back.  
- Find **Not Following**: Users you follow but who don’t follow you.  
- Upload **Instagram followers/following HTML files**.  
- Search and filter usernames.  
- Dark/Light mode toggle.  
- Reset button to start over.  
- Fully **client-side**, no data is sent to a server.  

---

## Pages

1. **Home Page** (`home.html`) – Landing page.  
2. **Upload Page** (`index.html`) – Upload your HTML files.  
3. **Results Page** (`result.html`) – View Fans & Not Following lists.  

---


## Usage

1. Open [Home Page](https://ldebnath.github.io/Instagram-Unfollowers-Finders/).  
2. Click to go to the upload page.  
3. Upload your followers & following HTML files.  
4. View results and search/filter usernames.  
5. Toggle dark/light mode at bottom-right.  
6. Use **Reset** to clear stored data and start again.
